DestaHealth is the first of its kind, Mental Health Support Organization (MHSO). As a MHSO, DestaHealth provides a platform for mental health practice-owners to offload the business-administrative aspects of their treatment facilities. Mental health clinicians and providers contract with DestaHealth to provide professional business and practice management services. As a result, clinicians and providers are able to focus on their patient community and provide the highest standards of care.
